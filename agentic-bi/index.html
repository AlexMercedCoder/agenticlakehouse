The AI Analytics Paradox

Why connecting AI to enterprise data has been a complex systems integration nightmare.

Locked in Silos

Valuable insights are locked away in disparate silos: S3, Snowflake, Postgres. Moving it all to one place is slow, expensive, and creates stale copies.

Language Barrier

AI models don't speak "Enterprise". Without business context, they fail to answer questions like "What was last quarter's churn?" accurately.

Latency Friction

Agentic flow requires conversational speed. Traditional pipelines and warehouse queries are too slow to maintain a fluid dialogue with your data.
