1. Open, Interoperable, and Truly Free

It's not just branding. Dremio provides a fully managed, production-grade Apache Polaris based lakehouse catalog service that supports the Iceberg REST Catalog spec. Use Spark, Trino, or Flink to query your data.

  • $0 for the Catalog
  • $0 for API Calls

2. Radical "Bring Your Own"

Embrace flexibility. Dremio can provide, or you can bring your own.

  • • Bring Your Own Compute (Spark, etc.)
  • • Bring Your Own Storage (S3)

  • • Bring Your Own Catalog (Glue, Polaris, etc.)

3. Simplified Governance

Built-in Multi-Tenancy. Each Dremio Organization acts as a distinct Apache Polaris Realm, and each project in an organization acts as a distinct Apache Polaris catalog, providing clean isolation for Prod, Dev, or different teams with fine-grained security.
